about

Sri Vasavi Foundry was established in 1997, with the vision of becoming one of the leading manufacturer of high quality sand castings. Sri Vasavi Foundry Private Limited is an ISO 9001:2015 certified Company for manufacturing and supplying of High-Chrome, Gray Iron, SG Iron & Other Alloy Iron Castings.

The company is engaged in manufacturing and supply of Castings in Various grades / sizes and weights up to 120 Kg per piece to meet Indian & International standards like IS & ASTM and any other customer requirements.

Our Company is well equipped with Moulding, Melting, Fettling Facilities and Spectro Lab. The Foundry has experienced team of engineers and staff.

Vasavi Foundry is mainly Specialized in Manufacturing of High-Chrome Castings, Spare Parts of Crushers and Concrete Mixers etc.

Our major customers are M/s. Schwing Stetter (India) Pvt. Ltd., M/s. Proman Infrastructure Services Pvt Ltd, M/s. JSW Steel Ltd., M/s. Allied Engineering, M/s. Belvik Infratech Systems, etc.,
